About Us

At Gammageeks, we pride ourselves on merging technical expertise with visionary ideas.

We Are GammaGeeks

Gammageeks, a company built on innovation, creativity, and a passion for technology and business. Our diverse portfolio reflects our commitment to providing unique, quality products and services that cater to modern needs.
MyrootShop.store is our vibrant e-commerce platform powered by Shopify, where we offer an exclusive collection of T-shirts and home decor inspired by African culture, heritage, and contemporary trends. Our goal is to bring meaningful designs to life, allowing our customers to express their individuality while enhancing their living spaces with stylish decor

On the other hand, KigaLuxe.estate is our real estate venture, connecting buyers, sellers, and renters with luxury properties in Kigali and beyond. We provide a seamless online experience for those looking to purchase or rent premium real estate, offering a curated selection of homes and plots tailored to your lifestyle and preferences.
